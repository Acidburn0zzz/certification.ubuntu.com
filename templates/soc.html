Ubuntu System on a Chip certified hardware

Ubuntu SoC certification guarantees that SoC platforms are supported in standard Ubuntu releases. Our engineering teams work with SoC vendors to include the necessary hardware enablement code, stabilize the core system, and verify specific applications and workloads function optimally on the platform. Certification is annual and covers two Ubuntu releases for their support duration.

Certified SoC platforms can be used in OEM systems participating in Ubuntu Certification programs. SoC not on our list? Contact us for more information on how the SoC Certification program can help you.
